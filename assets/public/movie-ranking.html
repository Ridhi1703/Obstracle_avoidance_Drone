The Best Movie According To Riddhi

My Top #3 Movies Of All-Time.

3 Idiots

It tells the story of Farhan and Raju, who form a bond with Rancho due to his positive outlook on life in college. Years later, a bet gives them a chance to look for their long-lost friend whose existence seems rather elusive. The movie is considered a masterpiece and easily one of the greatest Bollywood movies of all time.

Bahubali

The movie Baahubali has received positive reviews from critics.0 Roger Ebert praised the action choreography, the operatic plot, and the sense of optimism that undercuts the gouts of blood and thunderous sound effects.

IT

I had extremely high expectations that only increased when I heard about all the great reviews. And it's by no means a bad movie, but it certainly is a bit of a disappointment. The story isn't very faithful to the book, but it mostly keeps to the spirit of the novel and contains the essential elements. Adapting that book onto the screen is no easy job, so I'm gonna be a bit forgiving.
